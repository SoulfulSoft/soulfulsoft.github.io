## Contact Us

Thank you for using SoulfulSoft apps.
Please feel free to contact us with any feedback, feature requests, or bug reports.

### Feedback & Feature Requests

* Desired features
* Usability issues
* General impressions

Please feel free to send us your thoughts.

### Bug Reports

When reporting a bug, it would be helpful if you could include the following details:

* App name
* App version
* Android version
* Device model
* Description of the issue

### Regarding Replies

We aim to reply whenever possible,
but please note that we may not be able to respond depending on the circumstances or the nature of the inquiry.
